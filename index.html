Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to provide and improve the service, including:

  • Account and profile: Firebase Authentication user ID, display name/email (when provided by your sign-in provider), skin type, age range, and skin concerns.
  • Product activity: Barcodes you scan, products you view, favorites, ratings, and recent scans stored locally on your device.
  • Contributions: Product photos, ingredient list photos, ingredient text, and corrections you submit.
  • Diagnostics and analytics: App events, performance metrics, crash logs, and device/OS details to troubleshoot issues and improve stability.
  • Advertising data (Google AdMob): Advertising ID, ad interactions (impressions and clicks), device information, and coarse location (for example, city/region) for ad delivery and measurement.
  • Subscription data: Purchase status and entitlement information provided by the Apple App Store/Google Play and RevenueCat.

We do not collect: Precise location, medical records, biometric identifiers, or payment card numbers. Payments are processed by the Apple App Store or Google Play.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used to:

  • Provide ingredient analysis, product insights, and personalized content.
  • Manage your account, favorites, ratings, and contribution history.
  • Operate rewarded ads and subscription entitlements.
  • Improve the accuracy of our product database and app experience.
  • Detect, prevent, and investigate fraud or abuse.

3. Device Permissions

We request access to:

  • Camera: To scan product barcodes.
  • Photos/Storage: To upload product or ingredient photos for contributions.

You can manage permissions in your device settings.

4. AI & Automated Processing (xAI/Grok and Google Cloud Vision)

We use AI and OCR to analyze ingredient information:

  • xAI (Grok models): Ingredient lists and product context may be processed by xAI to generate summaries and insights. This processing is governed by xAI's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Cloud Vision OCR: Ingredient list photos may be processed to extract text. This processing is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • No PII Transmission: We do not send personally identifiable information (such as name or email) to AI or OCR providers. Only product-related content is shared.

Free users may see ads served by Google AdMob, including rewarded ads for credits. AdMob may collect or receive your advertising ID, device information, coarse location, and ad interaction data to show and measure ads. You can control ad personalization through your device settings (for example, by limiting ad tracking or resetting your advertising ID).

We do not sell personal data for monetary consideration. Some jurisdictions may consider the use of ad identifiers for personalized ads a "sale" or "sharing." You can limit this through your device settings.

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy and AdMob policies.

6. Analytics & Crash Reporting

We use Firebase Analytics to understand app usage and Firebase Crashlytics to diagnose crashes. These services may collect device identifiers, app events, and technical diagnostics as described in Firebase's Privacy Policy.

7. Data Sharing

We share data only with trusted service providers that help operate the app:

  • Google Firebase: Authentication, Firestore, Storage, Cloud Functions, Analytics, Crashlytics.
  • Google AdMob: Advertising delivery and measurement.
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management and entitlement status.
  • xAI (Grok) and Google Cloud Vision: AI analysis and OCR processing.
  • Apple/Google: In-app purchase processing and subscription management.

We may also share information if required by law, to protect user safety, or in connection with a business transfer (such as a merger or acquisition).

8. Data Storage & Security

We use Google Firebase (Firestore, Storage, and Cloud Functions) to securely store and process data. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal profile data is removed. Anonymized or aggregated contribution data (such as corrected ingredient lists and product photos) may be retained to maintain database integrity. Recent scans are stored locally on your device and can be cleared by deleting app data.

10. User Rights

Depending on your location (including GDPR for EU users), you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and personal data.
  • Opt out of personalized ads via your device settings.

You can exercise these rights in the app settings or by contacting us.

11. International Transfers

Your information may be processed and stored in countries other than your own, including the United States, where our service providers operate. We rely on appropriate safeguards for such transfers.

12. Children's Privacy

PureSkin is not intended for children under the age of 13 (or 16 in certain jurisdictions). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we become aware that we have collected such data, we will take steps to delete it immediately.
