Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal data necessary to provide our services:

  • Account Information: If you create an account, we store your User ID (via Firebase Authentication). We do not store passwords; authentication is handled securely by Google/Firebase.
  • Product Data: Barcodes you scan, photos of products or ingredients you upload, and any text corrections you submit.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous, aggregated analytics about how you interact with the app (e.g., screens visited, buttons clicked) to help us improve performance.
  • Device Information: Device type and operating system version for troubleshooting and optimization.

We DO NOT collect: Medical records, biometric data, precise location data, or payment card details (payments are processed entirely by the Apple App Store or Google Play Store).

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used to:

  • Provide ingredient analysis and product insights.
  • Manage your account and sync your scan history.
  • Improve the accuracy of our product database through crowd-sourced contributions.
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse of the service.

3. AI & Automated Processing (xAI / Grok)

PureSkin uses artificial intelligence services provided by xAI (Grok models) to process and analyze ingredient information.

You acknowledge and agree to the following data usage:

  • Model Improvement: Product-related data sent to xAI (such as ingredient text lists, OCR outputs, and anonymized product metadata) may be processed in accordance with xAI’s Privacy Policy and may be used by xAI to evaluate, train, or refine their AI models to improve performance and reliability.
  • No PII Transmission: PureSkin does not transmit personally identifiable user information (PII) to xAI. Only product-specific content is shared for analysis.

4. User-Generated Content

When you submit photos, new products, corrections, or other content to PureSkin ("Contributions"):

  • Public Availability: By submitting product images, ingredient photos, corrections, or other content to PureSkin, you acknowledge and agree that this content may be reviewed, modified, stored, and made publicly available within the application after administrative approval.
  • Database Improvement: User-submitted content may be used to improve the overall quality, accuracy, and completeness of the PureSkin product database for the benefit of all users.
  • Review Process: All contributions are subject to administrative review. We reserve the right to reject or modify content that does not meet our standards.

5. Data Storage & Security

We use Google Firebase (Firestore, Storage, and Cloud Functions) to securely store and process data. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account data as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal profile data is removed. However, anonymized product contributions (such as corrected ingredient lists and product photos) will be retained in our database indefinitely to ensure service continuity for other users.

7. User Rights

Depending on your location (including GDPR for EU users), you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and personal data.

You can exercise these rights directly within the app settings or by contacting us.

8. Children's Privacy

PureSkin is not intended for children under the age of 13 (or 16 in certain jurisdictions). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we become aware that we have collected such data, we will take steps to delete it immediately.
